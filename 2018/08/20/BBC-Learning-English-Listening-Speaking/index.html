BBC Learning English: Listening&Speaking [Pro]

This is the best English Learning App to learn english through simple English conversations: 6 Minute English, English At Work, The English We Speak…

This App helps you to improve your English Listening, English Grammar and English Speaking naturally.

This App also supports you with add-on English dictionary and auto scroll transcript.

软件简介

BBC LEARNING ENGLISH

  • Learn English through podcasts: 6 Minute English, LingoHack, Drama, Word in the news…
  • Each lesson comes with audio, transcript and vocabulary list, which help you to improve your English Grammar, English Listening and expand your Vocabulary.

PRACTICE ENGLISH

  • Test your English through fun Quiz and Test.
  • Learn English by chatting with other learners. You can learn Grammar, practice your English Speaking by voice recognition when chatting.

Favorite Features:

  • 3 Levels: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced
  • 9 categories with audio, transcript, exercise and quiz.
  • Free download to use when you’re offline.
  • Support dictionary.
  • Chat with other learners from all around the world.
  • Auto update new lessons.
