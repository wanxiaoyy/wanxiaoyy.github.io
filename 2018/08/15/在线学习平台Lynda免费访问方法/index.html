Lynda.com是什么？

我刚听说Lynda的时候我也不知道是什么？当时我只知道C站，也就是Coursera，还有就是什么edx以及国内的几家，根本没听说过Lynda。


Lynda.com is a leading online learning platform that helps anyone learn business, software, technology and creative skills to achieve personal and professional goals. Through individual, corporate, academic and government subscriptions, members have access to the Lynda.com video library of engaging, top-quality courses taught by recognized industry experts.

For 20 years, Lynda.com has helped students, leaders, IT and design pros, project managers—anyone in any role—develop software, creative, and business skills. Now part of Linkedin, Lynda.com serves more than 10,000 organizations. With tutorials in five languages, Lynda.com is a global platform for success.

我查了之后发现里面的课程质量还挺高，所以就找账号去学习一些课程。

今天教程注册的账号价值好多好多钱的。

好了我们现在开始今天的教程吧。

怎么获得 Lynda Premium Account

本教程用到的几个网址

  • http://www.freelibrary.org/
  • https://www.fakenamegenerator.com
  • https://temp-mail.org

打开freelibrary

打开FreeLibrary，点击右上角Get a library card

进入以下页面，点击online

A Free Library card is available at no cost to anyone who lives, works, pays taxes, or goes to school in the City of Philadelphia. In addition, anyone who lives in the state of Pennsylvania can obtain a Free Library card without charge.
Your library card will be valid for 60 days until you verify your account.
After verification, you can request a physical card by visiting any of our library locations.
Out-of-state residents can obtain a card by paying an annual fee of $50.00. Out-of-state residents must apply online and pay with a credit card.
You will be asked for identification the first time you borrow physical materials with your new card.
Suspected fraudulent online registrations can be deleted without prior notice. If you encounter any difficulty using this form, please contact us.

然后勾选I am 13 years of age or older ，点击Next。

关键的步骤

这个时候我们打开fake-name和临时邮箱

将对应的信息复制粘贴到freelibrary注册页面中，其中的Email Address使用临时邮箱。全部填写完了之后，在注册页面的最下方有个测试机器人的选框，记得勾选，然后就可以点击下一步了。

如果出现以下错误：

我们就去这里猛戳，随便选一个library的地址和邮编填进去就可以了。

之后我们就成功得到一个卡号了。

或者去我们的临时邮箱，里面有封邮件中也有卡号和你自己设置的PIN码。

登录Lynda.com

现在我们得到了卡号和PIN码，就可以去Lynda.com登录了。

但是这个账号登录的网址还不是普通的https://www.lynda.com， 而是这里。

进去后填写你的姓名和邮箱就可以开始学习了。
